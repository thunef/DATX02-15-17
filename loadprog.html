Uploading your program to PedaGogo

Step 1 Insert the USB-flash drive that comes with the PedaGoGo into your personal computer


Step 2 Press "Save Project" from the top bar file menu.


Step 3 Locate and enter the USB-flash drive (inserted in step 3) with the name of "PEDAGOGO"


Step 4 Replace the "drive.sb2" project file located here with the new project file, also called "drive.sb2".


Step 5 Remove the USB-flash drive from the personal computer and insert it into one of the USB-hubs on the PedaGoGo robot.


Step 6 Press the red button to load your project on the PedaGoGo and stop any other scripts currently running on the robot.


Step 7 Press one of the button(s) with the same color of a button block used in your script.


You can also load a project from the memory-stick, so you can pick up where you left off last time without having to redo your program!
